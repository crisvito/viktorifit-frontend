Do I need gym equipment to follow the training programs? +

No. Viktorifit provides both home-based and gym-based programs. You can choose according to your available equipment.

Viktorifit uses your profile data, goals, and activity history to recommend workouts that match your fitness level.

You can reschedule or continue your program without penalties. Viktorifit adapts to your progress, not perfection.

Yes. We prioritize user privacy and ensure your data is securely stored and protected.

Can beginners use Viktorifit? +

Absolutely. Viktorifit is designed for all levels, from beginners to advanced users.

Viktorifit offers free features, with optional premium plans for advanced programs and personalized recommendations.
